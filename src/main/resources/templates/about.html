About Page

Here at Aldani Board Games, we wish to spread the love and enjoyment of board games at an affordable price.

Our goal is to ensure that South Texas residents are able to foster enjoyable memories with one another, and help spread happiness and joy!

Currently, the boardgames that we offer are Chess, Checkers, Go, Monopoly and Scrabble. We definitely have plans to add more in the future, so please look forward to that!
